Entry 5: A funeral

(Trumpets, more trumpets)

清明
15°
All I remember was trumpets, so loud, I heard it in my dreams
A funeral of someone I didn't really know - only vague faces and impressions
My grandpa had more matters to take care of, and I cried, looking for him
I was worried, he would be gone too
His mother was gone, but I had barely known her
And tears
more tears
and standing
and shushing
and an emptiness
that accompanies death

Memories

  • The only funeral I've been to, been allowed to go to
